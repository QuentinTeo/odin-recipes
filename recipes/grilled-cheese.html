Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A simple comfort meal that can be easily be made and fast too. It only consists of bread, cheese and butter.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese (Cheddar and Mozzarella woukd do)
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place the ground beef in a skillet with olive oil.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
